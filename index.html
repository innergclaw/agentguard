How it works

Three layers between your agent and disaster.

1

Agent Proposes

Instead of executing directly, your agent emits a structured action proposal with context, target, and intent.

2

Guard Evaluates

Policy engine checks rules, risk scorer classifies the action, and decides: auto-approve, auto-deny, or escalate.

3

Action Executes

Approved actions run. Denied actions are logged. Escalated actions pause until a human decides. Full audit trail.

Everything you need to ship agents safely

📋

Policy Engine

Define rules in simple YAML — auto-approve reads, deny production deletes, escalate external emails.

⚡

Risk Scoring

Every action gets a risk classification — low, medium, high — based on impact, target, and context.

🔔

Human Escalation

Medium/high-risk actions pause and ping you via Slack, Discord, or email. One-click approve or deny.

📊

Audit Log

Every action ever proposed, approved, or denied — full trail for compliance and debugging.

🔌

Simple API

One POST request to intercept any action. Works with LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGPT, or custom agents.

⏱️

No human response in time? Action auto-denies. Safe by default, not sorry after the fact.
